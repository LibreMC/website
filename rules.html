Rules

Staff always have the final say in punishments

General

Use common sense

No use of hacked clients or client-side cheats which give you a gameplay advantage over others (Optifine et al. are allowed)

Do not attempt to cause lag on the server

Do not exploit a bug or vulnurability, instead report it to us

Do not exceed the limit of 2 alt accounts per player

PvP is only allowed in designated PvP areas

Creating gold farms is not allowed


Towny

Do not claim near another town in a way which blocks the other town from expanding or with the intention to ruin the surrounding area

Do not make odd town claims in a way which is unappealing (long one-block claims, disconnected areas, large holes...)

Town and nation names should be atleast partially realistic and sensical (eg. "Blablabla" is not a good name, while "Blatown" is)

Do not steal from a nation or town bank

Stealing from a chest in claimed land is allowed

Do not kill or steal mobs and other entities in claimed land

Do not grief in claimed or unclaimed land


Chat

Do not spam the chat

Do not advertise any service or content which is unrelated to the server

Do not harrass others, post slurs or other inflammatory content

Do not post links to websites which contain inflammatory or NSFW content
