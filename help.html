Beginner's guide

Travelling

First, check out our map to see where you are. Your visibility is based on light-levels, so if you're in a cave or it's night time you won't see yourself. Once you know where you are you should figure out which part of the world you wanna go to. You can teleport to towns using /t spawn NAME, where NAME is the name of the town.

Towns and nations

This server uses Towny, a server plugin which allows you to create towns and nations. You can either join or create a town. Towns can then join or create nations. To setup a town you need 50 gold. Once you have enough gold you can use /t create NAME where NAME is the name of your town. You'll need to pay a small sum for upkeep every 24 hours, which you can see by doing /t and look at the "upkeep" section.

To pay for your upkeep and costs of claiming more land you need to deposit it into your town's bank using /t deposit AMOUNT, where AMOUNT is the amount of gold you want to deposit

To claim more land you do /t claim in a chunk you haven't claimed yet. Each chunk costs 12 gold to claim until you reach your claim limit, which you can also see using /t. You can get a higher limit by either buying bonus chunks or by getting more players in your town

Obtaining gold

The primary way of obtaining gold is by mining. The gold deposits are based off IRL deposits, so you'll find more gold there where there is gold in real life. You can also mine in the nether, however the amount of gold is smaller and you can't teleport to any towns while in the nether
